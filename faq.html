How do I use Volufiline 100%?

Apply 2-3 drops to target areas (cheeks, nasolabial folds, under eyes) after cleansing and toning. Gently pat until absorbed. Use morning and night for best results. Can be mixed with your moisturizer for easier application.

Can I use Niacinamide 20% with other active ingredients?

Yes, Niacinamide 20% is compatible with most skincare ingredients including hyaluronic acid, peptides, and ceramides. However, we recommend not using it simultaneously with Vitamin C to avoid potential irritation. You can use Vitamin C in the morning and Niacinamide at night.

Is Bakuchiol safe during pregnancy?

Bakuchiol is a plant-based alternative to retinol and is generally considered safe during pregnancy. However, we always recommend consulting with your healthcare provider before introducing new skincare products during pregnancy or breastfeeding.
